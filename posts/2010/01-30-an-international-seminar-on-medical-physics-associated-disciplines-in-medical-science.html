Proposed Program:

 

1st Day

The first day of the program will address the following topics split into three sessions.

1.      Status and Development of Medical Physics

2.      Radiation Oncology and Radiation Oncology Medical Physics

3.      General : Nuclear Science and Medicine, Health Physics, Radiation Physics-Chemistry-Biology,  Radiation protection, Physics

1.Workshop on Medical Physics and Instrumentation

2. Pleasure trip.
