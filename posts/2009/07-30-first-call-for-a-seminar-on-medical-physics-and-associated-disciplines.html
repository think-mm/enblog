First Call for a Seminar on Medical Physics and Associated Disciplines

Published on Thursday, 30 July 2009

 

FIRST CALL FOR A SEMINAR

ON

MEDICAL PHYSICS AND ASSOCIATED DISCIPLINES

 

NOTICE

 

Call for Papers 

A two day Seminar on Medical Physics and associated disciplines will be held within the period of April 2010 – May 2010. The seminar is being organized by the Department of Medical Physics and Biomedical Engineering (MPBME), Gono Bishwabidyalay (University). An adhoc organizing committee has been constituted with : 

Professor Meshbaudin Ahmed, Vice-chancellor of the university: Patron 

Professor Ajoy K. Roy, Head of the Department of Medical Physics and Bio-medical Engineering: Convener

Tel: 880-2-935 9063 (R)

Email: roya_k2003@yahoo.com                       

Dr. Hasin Azhari, Senior Lecturer, Department of Medical Physics and Bio-medical Engineering department of Medical Physics and Bio-medical Engineering: Secretary

Tel: 880-2-811 8577 (R), 01711841063 (cell) 

and 10 other members.

The Seminar will address various topics on Medical Physics split into four sessions. 

Proposed Program: 

1st Day

The first day of the program will address the following topics split into three sessions.

  • Status and Development of Medical Physics

  • Radiation Oncology and Radiation Oncology Medical Physics

  • General : Nuclear Medicine, Health Physics, Radiation protection, Physics

 

2nd day

  • Workshop on Medical Physics and Instrumentation

  • Pleasure trip.

 We cordially invite you to participate in the seminar. Last date for submitting the abstracts of the papers/invited talks is February 15, 2010. Please get in touch with the Convener/ Secretary for further information.  

Hope you will kindly extend your helping hand and cooperation, especially in the financial matter to hold the seminar.
